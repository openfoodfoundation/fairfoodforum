Growers' Field Trip to Local Farms

The team at Lower Murray Water invites our irrigators and interested community members to visit local farms and agricultural businesses within the Mildura region who will host you on site to learn more about innovative practices, water efficiency and crop diversification.

The tour will be of particular interest to growers and investors looking to expand their business or looking to diversify crop types. Plus anyone interested in gaining an insight into current innovations in agriculture.

Included on the tour:

  • Opportunity to speak to various growers
  • View properties offering diverse crop types including pistachios, walnuts, cherries, avocados, mangos and seasonal vegetables.

Our tour bus will depart from our Mildura office and attendees are required to book their seat on the bus via the ticket link below.

Tickets are free and we ask attendees to arrive 15 minutes prior to departure time.

Lunch will be provided.

Bookings close by COB Wednesday 24 May 2017.
