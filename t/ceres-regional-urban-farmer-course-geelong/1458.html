CERES East Brunswick’s Urban Farmer goes regional! After running this popular program locally for ten years, our first regional Urban Farmer course commences in Newtown, Geelong on 19th October. This course combines 8 sessions including an introduction to permaculture, soils, propagation, fruit trees and more, and runs from 9am - 3pm. Suitable for those with a hobby farm or urban plot. Spaces are limited so book yourself in and get your hands dirty!
