DEMO Local food policy discussion forum, Perth City Council, WA

Announcements
admin1 February 17, 2016, 5:18am #1

The Perth City Council is hosting a discussion forum and is welcoming stakeholders to participate and share their views regarding how the local food policy should be shaped for 2017-2022.

It would be great to see as many representatives from the local food, the slow food and farmer’s markets movements attend. This includes customers, market operators and of course producers.

When: December 13th 2016
Where: Perth Town Hall
RSVP: localfood@perth.gov.au
