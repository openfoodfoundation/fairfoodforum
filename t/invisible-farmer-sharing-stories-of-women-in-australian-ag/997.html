Invisible Farmer is the largest ever study of Australian women on the land. This three year project is funded by the Australian Research Council and involves a nation-wide partnership between rural communities, academics, government and cultural organisations.

I posted an ad for a PhD scholarship for this project previously, but was just having a read through some of their featured stories - check it out: https://invisiblefarmer.net.au/featured-stories/

You can also submit a story: https://invisiblefarmer.net.au/share-a-story
