About the Livestock category

Farming Livestock
adminKL August 17, 2016, 5:48am #1

This category is the place for all discussions about livestock farming. This is the spot to talk about farm infrastructure, breeding plans and breed choices, feed choices, and more.

If you want to talk about processing or distribution of animal products, those discussions are better suited to other categories. Check out the Processing category, or for distribution discussions check out the Connecting category which covers sub-categories such as retail, CSAs, food hubs, and more.
