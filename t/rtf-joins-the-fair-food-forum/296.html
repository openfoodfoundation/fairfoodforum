RTF joins the Fair Food Forum

Food Justice
luke.craven May 2, 2017, 6:55am #1

Hi everyone,

I’m excited that the Right to Food Coalition will be actively participating in the Fair Food Forum. Our aim is to use it as our ‘community of practice’, as a place for discussion and exchange, and to share news, events and resources.

This category ‘Food Justice’ is for the entire Right to Food conversation, and we have established two sub-categories for those working or interested in community food security and the charitable food sector.

Please do join in the discussion!

Luke.
