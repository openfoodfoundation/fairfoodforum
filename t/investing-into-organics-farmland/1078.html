Are you an ORICoop member yet? Are you ready for the launch of our very first Investment Offering into a certified organic farm in Victoria?
All ORICoop members will have the first option to invest, to be released later this week. The ORICoop Board and all members are super excited about this pioneering moment for ORICoop!

Thursday 5pm - come on an organic farm tour! See this certified farm in it’s current form, hear from Frank about his vision, and see the vast opportunities that it will bring to this region. Think hemp, local grains, market garden crops, and many more options!
Thursday 6:30pm - Marong Hotel (near Bendigo) come and hear all the details about the farm, meet the farmer, and meet other ORICoop members in the fabulous local, community minded area of Bendigo region. (see invite to book)
