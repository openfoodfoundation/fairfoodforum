2018 Women On Farms Gathering

Announcements EVENT
farming, victoria
ssaxton August 11, 2017, 5:27am #1

The committee for the 29th Women On Farms Gathering would like to invite any local women to join the committee in organising this really fun event and we would also like to invite the rest of Victoria’s farming women to come along!
The website and booking links are currently under construction but you can follow us on social media to stay up to date and register your interest via email.
We haev a young energetic committee this year and are planning a range of activities which will appeal to women of all ages. Think wine tasting, foodie masterclasses, farm gate tours and beach hikes!
