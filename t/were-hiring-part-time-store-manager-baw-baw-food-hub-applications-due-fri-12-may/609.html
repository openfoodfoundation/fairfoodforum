We're Hiring! Part time store manager Baw Baw Food Hub, applications due Fri 12 May

Announcements Jobs
victoria
bawbawfoodhub May 10, 2017, 6:20am #1

We’re hiring a new PART TIME STORE MANAGER at the Baw Baw Food Hub, Warragul, Victoria.

This role involves managing the Hub’s range of local and organic produce and our Tuesday afternoon shop, with the help of volunteers and other team members.

It’s an ongoing weekly part-time position (8 hours/week).

If you’re passionate about local, healthy, sustainable food and would like to be part of our dynamic team, please email info@bawbawfoodhub.org for a copy of the full Job Description or call Lynda on 0458 266 294 for more information.

Apply by letter telling us about your suitability for this role to the above email by Friday, 12th May 5.00pm.
