Food and Ag accelerator program- Applications open until August 31st 2017

I came across this accelerator program offered by a partnership between Monash Uni, The Food Innovation Centre, My Other Kitchen and Badalya.

Could be of interest to those looking for mentoring and support around a new venture…

Here’s their blurb…

“We work with entrepreneurs seeking to bring new products and services to market right across the value chain from production to consumption, including food and beverages, software and hardware, chemistry, equipment, packaging, new processes and new business models.You can be really early stage with a “cracker” of an idea, all the way to a minimum viable product or service with paying customers.”
