Australian Fair Food Forum

Survey for Planning Regulations Reform

Announcements
Ant November 3, 2017, 3:10am #1

AFSA has released an 8min survey to collect data about pastured livestock farmers.

It’s super important to get as many responses as possible so AFSA can make an accurate and detailed submission to Vic and NSW (and the rest of Aust when they try to follow suit). AFSA will be submitting a draft of what the regulations should look like along with a code of practice so take the survey to help make sure we capture all our farming comrades in our submission.

https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/AFSAlivestock

Please SHARE through all your networks ASAP as time is very limited and there’s a lot of work to do.
