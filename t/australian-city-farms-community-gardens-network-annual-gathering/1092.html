The Australian City Farms & Community Gardens Network (ACFCGN) organises an annual gathering of city farmers and community gardeners to share our stories, network and renew friendships.

This year it will be at CERES in Melbourne on Saturday 7 October 2017 and visiting gardens in the Geelong area on Sunday 8 October 2017.
