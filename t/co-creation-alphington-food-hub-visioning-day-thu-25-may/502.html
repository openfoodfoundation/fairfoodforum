From SUSTAIN newsletter

Inviting food system thinkers and food sustainability practitioners, from key organisations across municipalities, to co-create a shared vision and set of objectives for the activation of the Alphinton Food Hub site.

The outcomes of the day will inform the next steps of the project, including a detailed study for which support will be sort from the City of Darebin, and form the basis for a major grant application with the Lord Mayor’s Charitable Foundation.

When & where
The visioning day will be held on Thursday 25th of May, details TBC. To register your interest please contact info@sustainaustralia.org.
