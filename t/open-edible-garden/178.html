Karen Sutherland’s Gunyah Garden Open Garden Weekend
March 4 @ 10:00 am - March 5 @ 4:30 pm
| $8
Karen Sutherland with Open Gardens Victoria is opening her indigenous and edible garden in Pascoe Vale South. Explore a variety of over 200+ edible plants growing in an intensive urban food forest utilised for food, medicinal purposes and beauty.

Open Saturday and Sunday 10am – 4pm. $8 entry, students and kids free.

For sale on the day (cash only): a selection of rare plants, honey from the rooftop bees, assorted garden items.

Funds raised are shared with West Brunswick Community Garden who will be helping on the gate and providing refreshments.
