Co-Working space available at Alphington Community Food Hub

Announcements
Prudence April 8, 2018, 11:46pm #1

Are you involved in primary production? Building a business around food? A local food organisation or community group?

Melbourne Farmers Markets (MFM) is excited to announce the establishment of a co-working space in Alphington that is part of a broader multi- stakeholder community food hub project. MFM invites individuals, organisations and businesses to submit their expression of interest in renting a commercial office space.

The co-working space will have access to WiFi, printing/scanning facilities, shared kitchen and amenities and is part of the of the Melbourne Innovation Centre, with business incubation and mentoring services available.

The location is 2 Wingrove Street, Alphington and just across the road from the Alphington Train Station. Parking is also available.

MFM is open to receiving interest for occasional, short-term, long-term, hot-desk arrangements and meeting rooms.

MFM is currently developing the framework and agreements for the co-working spaces, with tenancy scheduled for start of June 2018.

If you would like to see the space, or express interest, please email:

prue@mfm.com.au
