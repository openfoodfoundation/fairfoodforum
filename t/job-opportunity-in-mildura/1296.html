slff December 20, 2017, 10:17pm #1

Out of the Box is a weekly local produce box run by the not-for-profit Food Next Door. We are recruiting a 0.8FTE Operations Leader based in Mildura Victoria. Applications close 21 January for a start date of 28 January. You will be working as part of a team of young passionate people and need to be passionate about local food systems and supporting small-scale regenerative farmers. The role requires a broad range of skills including marketing, subscription management and relationship building. Email @slff or sunraysialocalfoodfuture@gmail.com for position description.
