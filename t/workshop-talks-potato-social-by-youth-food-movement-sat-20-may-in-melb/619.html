Proud to present to you our event created to teach young adults about our foodsystem!

Potato Social - a community celebration of the humble spud.

From: https://www.facebook.com/events/170689890122531/

The YFM Melbourne crew are throwing a party in the name of our most loved vegetable of the century! A tasty selection of workshops, tater talks and delicious feasting highlighting the faces, processes and edible adaptability of this starchy beast.

Your ticket to the Potato Social gets you access to three workshops, each shining light on a different chapter of the spud story, ending with a special Potato Social lunch.

WORKSHOP PROGRAM
Keep a keen eye out for the three workshop reveals.

  • Beyond Potatoes with Dr. Chris Williams - potato growing tater talk
  • Making Gnocchi - hands-on cooking workshop
    • Surprise workshop!

POTATO SOCIAL LUNCH to share

  • The Social Gnocchi
  • The Creamy Potato Leek Soup
  • The Potato Social Salad

Join us for this sweet (potato) event paying homage to all spud lovers, growers and eaters. Bring along a healthy appetite, a curious mind plus extra gold coins for the Potato Raffle with prizes from Rooftop Honey, The Melbourne Gin Company, Pip Permaculture Magazine and Bulleen Art & Garden
