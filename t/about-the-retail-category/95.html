About the Retail category

Connecting Retail
adminKL August 17, 2016, 6:01am #1

This category is the place for all discussions about food retail. This is the spot to talk about experiences selling food, marketing techniques, customer relations, things that are working, things that aren’t or which you have questions about, and more.

If you want to talk about processing in preparation for selling, those conversations are better suited to the Processing category, or for discussions about other specific forms of distribution check out the Connecting category which covers sub-categories such as food hubs, CSAs, and farmers’ markets.
