‘My Other Kitchen’ is the organiser of Let’s Talk information series held monthly. Monday 15 May the topic is Food Packaging.

Program
If you are making a food product for sale and have serious plans to see your product on a retail shelf or a national or international product, then you’ll need to consider the packaging. In particular, labels have a multi-purpose - to attract attention and make your customers want what’s inside the packet, to protect your product and keep it safe, to convey all the information your customers need to make a decision to buy.

Let’s talk about:
How to attract attention to your product on a retail shelf
The packaging and labeling laws
Looking good and being legal

Speaker:
Clare Blackstock, Blade Creative’s Director, graphic designer and all-round branding expert.
