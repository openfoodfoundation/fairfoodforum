Hi everyone, just wanted to spread the word about a new project we have just launched to try to make it easier than ever for agribusinesses to find, compare and hire the best sustainable agriculture consultants in the world.

https://agtalent.consulting/ is a website where anyone can request quotes from specialists, independent advisors and skilled freelancers who work broadly under the sustainable agriculture banner across the whole supply chain. Think drone pilots, organic transition consultants, product developers, agtech experts, researchers, marketing professionals, regenerative farming advisors and many more.

It takes 5 minutes to fill out the quote request. Then we will match the project to our growing list of consultants.

Would really love to hear from any consultants in Aus who are keen to join our network? And we are really open to ideas, thoughts and feedback.

Cheers,
