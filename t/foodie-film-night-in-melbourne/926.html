Foodie film night in Melbourne

Announcements EVENT
victoria
rebecca.lindberg July 7, 2017, 12:07am #1

Come along to Cinema Nova, Carlton, to watch a screening of Michael Pollan’s ‘In Defense of Food’ film.

Thursday July 20th. 6:30-8:30pm. $25.

There will be a Q and A with local food gurus after the film including Dr Seona Candy (Foodprint Melbourne) and Marcus Godinho (FareShare), plus raffle prizes and more!

All proceeds go towards advocacy and awareness-raising efforts by the volunteer not-for-profit organisation the Right to Food Coalition.
