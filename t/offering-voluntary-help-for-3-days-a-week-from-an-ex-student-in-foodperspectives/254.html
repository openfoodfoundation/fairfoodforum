Dear Fair Food Lovers,

I’d like to offer 3 days of help as a volunteer in the fair food sector!
Why do I offer this?
Since my study abroad at La Trobe in Food perspectives (nutritional, anthropological and gastronomical) my interest, excitement and curiosity in this area keeps growing and I hope to be of help to others with my gained knowledge and experience and at the same time learning more about fair food and the system behind it.
How do I do this?
The coming couple of months I’d like to dedicate about 3 days a week to volunteering. At the moment I am also a teamplayer at Youth Food Movement Melbourne.
What could I do?
The work could be very practical: helping out on a market, event, farm etc., at the same time helping with research or communications is something I’d like to help with (I have a degree in Communications)

A little bit more about me: I am originally from the Netherlands and after I got my degree in Communications I moved to Melbourne (about 8 months ago) to do a semester abroad and learn more about the different perspectives on food. I loved the semester and I am loving the foodvibe here in Melbourne, therefore I am not going home yet and hope to do and learn more about this here in Australia!

Hopefully via this forum we can get in contact and create a fruitful collaboration !
Thankyou already for any ideas and suggestions.

Warm regards,
Simone
