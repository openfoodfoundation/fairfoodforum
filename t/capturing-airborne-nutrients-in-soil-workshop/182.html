CAN – Capturing airborne nutrients in the soil
WHEN: Saturday March 11th 2017, 9.45am -2.00 pm – lunch provided
WHERE: Warrenbayne Hall, Dobson’s Rd, Warrenbayne
WHAT: To introduce the aims & aspirations, concepts and design of the project.
WHY: To explore the idea of “Grow your topsoil and Grow more grass”.
Dr John Russell (Latrobe University) will explain and illustrate the biology behind this method. Rob Hodges, Euroa, will share his experience of 10 years using this approach on his own farm. The day will include demonstrations & a site visit.

For more information, please contact Ethne Green on 0408 287 976 or email ethne.green@bigpond.com
RSVP (phone or email) by 9th March for catering purposes
