DEMO Demeter biodynamic green manure workshop- Mt Gambier, SA

Announcements
sallystead2 February 17, 2016, 3:15am #1

We are hosting a green manuring workshop at our property in Mount Gambier, SA. Demeter biodymanic farming advisor Susan Cash will be speaking about the principles of green manuring.
The event is open to the public and we encourage any interested landholders to come along for an informative and fun day on the farm. There’ll be a potluck dinner after the event, so bring along a plate to share if you’d like to continue the conversations into the evening.

When: Saturday 23rd of March 2016
Where:11 Holland Lane, Mt. Gambier, SA.
Cost: Free
Contact to RSVP: Bob Marsh, 040569223
