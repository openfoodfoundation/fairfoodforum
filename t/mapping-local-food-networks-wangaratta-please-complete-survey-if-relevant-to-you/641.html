SECTION A: CURRENT PRODUCTION
This questionnaire has been developed to assist in the mapping of the local food network in the Rural City of Wangaratta LGA. The Community Food for All Network (CFFA) aims to strengthen the local food system in Wangaratta. The CFFA wishes to connect with local producers to understand their production and marketing, their needs and their aspirations. This knowledge will be used to inform the work of the CFFA and assess the potential for a Food Hub in Wangaratta.
