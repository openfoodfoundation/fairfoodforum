Dal Zotto Wines and the International Specialised Skills Institute (ISS Institute) are offering one (1) international Fellowship in the amount of $12,500.

This Fellowship provides an opportunity for a resident of Victoria (Australia) to focus on innovative practices, skill development and leadership for social and economic development in regional Victoria. The aim of this Fellowship is to support a regional-based person to undertake international applied research in order to develop as professional, innovator and leader while also using new learnings and skills to contribute to their local community.

The proposed Fellowship can focus on any area worthy of international investigation and exploration but must be oriented towards driving social and economic innovation in regional Victoria. The Fellowship can be conducted in any part of the world.

Before applying for this Fellowship thoroughly read and review the Fellowship Application Guidelines and Fellowship Application Form to determine whether your proposal meets the criteria. Feel free to contact ISS Institute if you have any questions.

Applications close at 4:00pm on Friday 27 October, 2017
