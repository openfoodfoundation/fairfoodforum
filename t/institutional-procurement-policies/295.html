Hi all,

Together with @sophie.anne.jackson I’m working on the fairfoodchallenge which is aiming to improve the food environments on Australian university campuses.

For a couple of years we’ve been collecting together resources that can help us in building a fair food framework for procurement. I guess a lot of the models we’ve looked at overseas rely on more centralised procurement (i.e. American unis with dining halls and procurement officers) whereas here it is very decentralised.

It’d be great to gather together any resources, frameworks, suggestions or ideas from the community.

Cheers,

Sophie & Sophie
