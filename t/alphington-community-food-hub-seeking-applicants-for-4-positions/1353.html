Alphington Community Food Hub seeking applicants for 4 positions!

Announcements Jobs
Prudence April 17, 2018, 4:47am #1

Positions are now open for the various roles required to establish the trailblazing Alphington Community Food Hub that will be a place for everyone to create and connect to a healthy and flourishing local food system.

MFM is delighted to be partnered with Sustain: The Australian Food Network to deliver Australia’s first multi-stakeholder, multi-purpose community food hub. This initiative is made possible by funding through the Lord Mayor’s Charitable Foundation.

Food Hub Manager : applications close 20 April

Business Development Manager : applications close 24 April

Communications Coordinator : applications close 24 April

Urban Agricultural Facilitator : applications close 24 April

For more information please go to: http://www.circlesoffood.org/2018/04/10/alphington-food-hub-positions-advertised/
