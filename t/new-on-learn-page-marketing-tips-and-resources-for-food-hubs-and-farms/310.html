For everyone who is interested in learning more about the art of marketing: from the simple basics to more in-depth theories and on top some quick time-saving tools!

You can find the resource here on the Open Food Network Learn site, which gives tips provided by food and farm institutes like the CSA, agriculture departments and fellow farmers, including @SallyRuljancich tips on Social Media (from Colin and Sally’s Organic Lamb and Beef farm in South Gippsland).

Aside from all these resources, what better way than learning from the experience of others and your own. Feel free to talk here about the do’s, and also the don’ts, on marketing with fellow food partners in the network! @MelbFarmersMarkets, @emmy_grows and @SallyRuljancich, maybe you would like to share some more ideas and experience on this?
