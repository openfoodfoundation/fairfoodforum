Farmer Incubator helps to grow new farmers. We do this by providing non-traditional learning pathways via programs where participants can take a crop from seed to market. We have the 2017 Pop-Up Garlic Program that runs between March and December, which now has three spots available for a discounted price of $590 ($100 off!).

This is the third year for the garlic program, and we have 18 participants growing across 4 great sites! Spots are still available in Bundoora and Ballan. Check out our website for more info (farmerincubator.org) or email us at hello@farmerincubator.org. Workshops start soon so be quick!!!
