New resource: how to give interviews to media

Other Discussion
communications, resource
Jen April 12, 2017, 6:04am #1

For many community food enterprises a big part of what they do is trying to tell the story of the fair food system, and of their own enterprise.

There’s a new resource on the Open Food Network Learn site which gives tips on one step of the story-telling process: giving interviews to media. Check it out at https://openfoodnetwork.org/au/learn/resources/how-to-give-interviews-to-media/

Interested to hear of others’ experiences and lessons learned on this front. Have you learned something from your experiences with media and telling the story of fair food that you think would help others? Or do you have questions about interacting with media that you want others’ input on?
